Salt and Ions Water Treatment System was organized by the undersigned in 2005 in Cebu City. A duly organized company, Salt an Ions Water Treatment System is a proprietorship owned and operated by MR. Cornelio Balmaceda III.

Mr. Cornelio Balmaceda III had been in the swimming pool business since 1974, and started with Marina Pools Inc., where it served as its Construction Manager and later on as its President.

In 1985, Mr. Cornelio E. Balmaceda III, organized the family own swimming pool business known as Patricor Construction Inc. named after his children PATRICIA, TRICIA AND CORNELIO IV.

In the year 2005, of its incorporated opted to settle in the abroad thus PATRICOR ceased its operations. That same year, the undersigned then organized Salt an Ions Water Treatment System named after two gadgets for swimming pool water treatment namely SALT CHLORINATOR and IONIZER.

Mr. Balmaceda had also attended swimming pool seminars and conventions in Australia and the United States. He also attend the Water Park Convention in Florida and Beijing, China several years ago.

We have lost count on the number of swimming pools we have constructed, however one of the more popular projects that we undertook was the construction of the J Park Water Park formerly known as Imperial Palace Water Park located in Maribago, Mactan, Lapu-Lapu City and presently J Park Panglao Island Resort.

With our vast experiences and technical knowledge in swimming pool and water park construction, we can assure you of the best quality service and workmanship. We take care of our clients and we are proud to say that we extend our services and assistance even after completion of our project.

In 2009, we were commissioned by Family Fun pools owned by Mr. Scott Cook to exclusively sell/install their “UNIWALL” product here in the country. The Uniwall is made of fiberglass sheet. It comes is 120m to 200m rolls, and is manufactured in Cleveland Ohio USA. It comes in five [5] different colors and can be installed between 5– 7 days depending on the pool size/area.

The present we are constructing the waterpark in J Park Panglao Island Resort located is Panglao, Bohol. It will be using the “Uniwall” fiberglass walls.

The BUMPER BOAT at Snow World located in Minglanilla, Cebu and owned by Anjo World made use the “Uniwall” fiberglass walls and was recently completed. The projects can be inspected upon request.

In using the Uniwall fiberglass walls, the following are its advantages over concrete Pool:

  • a.

    Structure Design. Light weight and advantage on upper floor pools.

  • b.

    Minimal concrete requirements

  • c.

    Light material compared to concrete

  • d.

    Faster installation from 1– 2 weeks unlike concrete walls

  • e.

    Uniwalls are pre finished. No repairs, no waterproofing, formworks required

  • f.

    Concrete flooring can be concreted separately

The Uniwall comes in rolls between 150m – 200m. Its uniform width is 1.20.

A living example of the Uniwall quality is the J Park Mactan Water Park. It had been existing for almost 10 years without any repairs. It was able to survive the big earthquake in Cebu several years ago.

At present, we had been commissioned by the J Park Resort to undertake the construction of their water park facilities and several swimming pools. We have started with the 12 plunge pools for the Ocean Villas and 24 pools for their Family Villas. The project however is temporarily suspended due to the pandemic.

In July 2018, another company PLAVA VODA, located in Perrelos, Carcar City was formed with partnership with MR. WILSON BALMACEDA. This company deals more with the sales of swimming pools equipment and chemicals, offers consulatncy, and repairs.
